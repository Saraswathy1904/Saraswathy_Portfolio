Introduction

Kuberlearn is the new learning platform that contains 'App Development' based courses in Tamil. The courses are conducted by the well-trained instructors. This platform is also built to Offer internship opportunities for students.

My Role

I led the design, user testing and logo of this project from end to end for both mobile and desktop. I collaborated with a developer during the early ideation stage.

Problem - Language Barrier

Over 50% of the students are interested in learning "App development" as it is one of the trending profession today. But the students find it difficult to understand and cope up with the language.


They feel helpless when stuck up with doubts, while language is the barrier to raise their questions.


Not getting proper Internship opportunities to start their carrier as Developers.

Goal

To create a learning platform that bridges the gap bewteen tamil students and coding knowledge.


Mentors should guide the students and solve their doubts in Tamil or English based on the student.


Students should get Internship opportunities to start their carrier as Developers.


The platform must be user friendly with decent Interface.

User Testing

Done User-testing with my friends and family who has experience in purchasing the courses from several learning platforms.


I got positive responses for the below questions I asked to them :


Can you describe what this app is all about?


Is the app user-friendly?


How is the Interface looks like?


Any suggestions to change anything?
